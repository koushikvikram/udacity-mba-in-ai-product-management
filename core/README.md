# Core Curriculum

Required foundation — **625 credit hours** across 5 module groups.

Complete these before or in parallel with electives (check your Udacity learning plan for the official sequence).

| # | Module | Hours | Folder |
|---|--------|-------|--------|
| 1 | Implementation & Product Introduction | 250 | [01-implementation-and-product-introduction/](./01-implementation-and-product-introduction/) |
| 2 | Statistics & Data Analysis | 175 | [02-statistics-and-data-analysis/](./02-statistics-and-data-analysis/) |
| 3 | Digital Marketing | 125 | [03-digital-marketing/](./03-digital-marketing/) |
| 4 | Growth Product Management | 100 | [04-growth-product-management/](./04-growth-product-management/) |
| 5 | Business Leadership & Strategy | 75 | [05-business-leadership-and-strategy/](./05-business-leadership-and-strategy/) |

## Suggested study order

Udacity may unlock modules in a specific order on your learning plan. A logical progression for product-focused learners:

1. **Product Manager** → **AI Product Manager** (build PM + AI PM foundation)
2. **Intro to Statistics** → **Inferential Statistics** → **BI Analytics** (data literacy)
3. **AI for Business Leaders** → **Digital Transformation** (strategy context)
4. **Digital Marketing** → **Growth Product Manager** (go-to-market & growth)

Adjust based on your background and RPL credits.

## Per-program folder structure

Each program folder contains:

```
program-name/
├── README.md      # Program overview & study workflow
├── SYLLABUS.md    # Lesson & project checklist
├── notes/         # Blank sheets — one per course ([Blank Sheet Method](../notes/templates/blank-sheet.md))
└── projects/      # Project reflection stubs (where applicable)
```

## Study workflow (all core programs)

1. **SYLLABUS.md** — check off completion
2. **Blank sheets** — prime, session log, revision schedule, synthesis
3. **Projects** — reflection on submit; post-mortem if resubmit/fail
4. **meta/** — bursts, weekly metrics, post-mortems ([operating model](../meta/epictetus-operating-model.md))

Re-run `python3 scripts/init_program_files.py` after curriculum updates to refresh syllabi (existing note/project files are preserved).

Update [../progress/TRACKER.md](../progress/TRACKER.md) as you complete programs.
