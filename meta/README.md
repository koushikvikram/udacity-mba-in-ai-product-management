**Layer 3** of this repo: how you *perform* while completing the MBA — not just what you learn or where you are in the syllabus.

The curriculum lives in `core/` and `progress/`. The operating model lives here.

## Daily minimum (Layer 1 spine)

Two non-negotiables — restore these before adding anything else:

1. **Short bursts** — 1–3 × 25–50 min; one concrete artifact per burst (notes entry, project progress, decision)
2. **Post-mortem within 24h** — any setback: failed quiz, rejected project, bad study session, missed week

Everything else (ditch-the-ladder, AI disagreements, inscrutability reps) compounds on top.

---

## Weekly minimum (Layer 3)

Every week (~15 min):

1. Update [metrics.md](./metrics.md)
2. Complete [weekly meta review](./templates/weekly-meta-review.md) (copy to `log/reviews/YYYY-MM-DD.md` if you want history)
3. Ask: *Where was I most predictable? Which loss did I skip post-mortem-ing?*

Monthly: change one variable in your "opening repertoire" — a new tool, study time, elective angle, or capstone framing.
