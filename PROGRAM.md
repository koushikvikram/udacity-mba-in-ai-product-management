# Program Overview

**Degree:** Master of Business Administration — specialization in AI Product Management  
**Awarding institution:** Woolf (via Udacity Institute of AI & Technology)  
**Format:** 100% online, self-paced  
**Total workload:** 2,250 credit hours (~14 Nanodegrees + capstone)

Sources: [program brochure](./udacity_mba_brochure.pdf), [Udacity program page](https://www.udacity.com/mba-ai-product-management), [Udacity Support — About the Program](https://support.udacity.com/hc/en-us/articles/44221586096909-About-the-Program)

---

## Why this program

- **Accredited MBA** recognized across 60+ countries (ECTS / Woolf)
- **Project-based** — portfolio-ready work, not exams-only
- **Affordable** — complete for under ~$5,000 USD (subscription + $199 enrollment fee)
- **Flexible** — no fixed semesters; pause subscription up to 90 days at a time
- **Backwards compatible** — prior Udacity Nanodegrees may count via Recognition of Prior Learning (RPL)

## Admissions & enrollment

### Prerequisites

- Bachelor's degree (recommended) or equivalent experience
- Performance-Based Admission (PBA) pathway available without a degree
- English proficiency
- Active Udacity subscription (required to apply)

### Process

1. Subscribe to Udacity All Access (~$249/month)
2. Pay one-time **$199 enrollment fee** (non-refundable; covers application, admission review, credit conversion)
3. Submit short application + ID verification
4. Have prior learning evaluated (RPL)
5. Start learning immediately upon approval (~1 business day)

### Subscription rules

- Must stay subscribed to remain in the degree program
- Cancel/unenroll ends participation; **progress is retained**
- Re-enrolling does **not** require paying the $199 fee again
- Pause subscription up to **90 days** at a time (removed from program during pause; reactivate to continue)
- After resuming, must stay active **≥1 month** before pausing again

---

## Recognition of Prior Learning (RPL)

If you previously completed eligible Udacity programs:

- Credit may apply toward the degree
- You may **not** need to retake the full Nanodegree
- You **will** need to complete **remediation**: academic reading + assessment per program

Document completed programs in [rpl/prior-learning.md](./rpl/prior-learning.md).
