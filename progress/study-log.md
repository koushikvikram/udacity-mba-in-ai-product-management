# Study Log

Track **short bursts** (25–50 min) and weekly hours. Part of the [Epictetus operating model](../meta/epictetus-operating-model.md): one artifact per burst.

> **Not the session log.** This file tracks *time and volume* (bursts, minutes, module). What you *learned* each session goes in the course blank sheet's [Session log](../notes/README.md#session-log) section (e.g. `core/.../notes/01-product-strategy.md`).

Copy a new week block at the top as you go.
